About Gyaan Tribe Global

Gyaan Tribe Global is a youth-led initiative focused on knowledge sharing, entrepreneurship, and cross-cultural collaboration. We create pathways for young people to learn, lead, and build livelihoods through workshops, mentorship, and community projects.

Our Mission

To empower global youth with skills, connections, and opportunities that enable them to become change-makers in their communities and beyond.

What We Do

  • Run skills workshops and bootcamps.
  • Support youth-led projects and ventures.
  • Build a global mentorship and peer-learning network.
